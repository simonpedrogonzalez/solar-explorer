How to Use:
• Scroll to zoom in.
• Use the slider to explore missions by launch date.
• Accurate Planet Positions sets planets positions according to date slider.
• Hover over objects and mission paths to reveal info.
• Click objects and missions to highlight them in both dashboards; click Clear Selection to reset.
• Click Switch Dashboard to switch between the Solar System Map and the Data Explorer.
